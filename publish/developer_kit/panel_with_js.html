Depending on the purchase, contracts were passing through 4 different desks for approval. To complicate matters, some departments had to sign twice: once to approve spend, and then later to review and approve contract terms. To transform their operations, procurement worked with the legal department to consolidate the contract and legal approval process. The combination of process improvement and integration of Adobe Sign resulted in a total contract cycle reduction of 73%. Using Adobe Sign, procurement team members can easily upload contract templates from a shared drive and add signature fields. By switching from ink signatures to electronic documents, procurement has reduced its consumption of paper and printing supplies by more than 80%.
